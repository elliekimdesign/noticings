The City Archive

An Archive of San Francisco
Volume IMay, 2026

Pinned is a project about San Francisco. Every so often, a question is posted. People answer in one sentence and pin that sentence to a real place in the city.

You can read every answer that has been left. You can leave your own. You cannot edit, reply to, or react to anyone else's. Over time the map fills with the small, ordinary things people remember about places — Sunday mornings, walks they take often, places they sat for a while. The city shows up the way people actually use it, not the way it appears on a postcard.

The format is borrowed from Learning to Love You More, a project by Harrell Fletcher and Miranda July.
