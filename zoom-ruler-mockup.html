Ruler · diamond — 6 variants

A is the original (reference). B–F are different minimal takes on the same diamond-on-ruler direction. All same visual size, all positioned 60% along to show the indicator state.

AOriginal · ruler + 5 marks + diamond
Reference. Solid diamond head, 5 vertical marks along a horizontal line.
BBare · 3 ticks + diamond, no line
Strip the line. Just three taller ticks (start / middle / end) and the floating diamond. Most reductive.
COutline diamond · clean line
Continuous line, no tick marks, larger outline diamond. Looks like a needle gauge — quiet but precise.
DEnd-caps · soft middle line
Two strong vertical end-caps, a faded middle line, diamond floats. Reads like a measuring span.
EWith floating zoom number
Original ruler + tiny mono "12.5" hovering above the diamond. Adds info without clutter.

FTwo diamonds · zoom range
Two diamonds on the same line — one faded (min reached), one solid (current). Suggests a zoomable range.
