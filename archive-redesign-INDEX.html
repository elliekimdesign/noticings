Archive Rail

Four directions for the left archive column. Each is a standalone copy of the page, only the rail block differs. Pick a favorite (or a hybrid).

  • A Timeline400 px · serif Documentary-poster vertical rule. Past list as a chronology with Instrument Serif titles, mono numbers, year markers on the right edge.
  • B Jost color360 px · jost Pale blue field, orange accents on issue numbers, Jost typeface throughout. The rail reads like a magazine cover paired with the map.
  • C Hairline rules360 px · grid Swiss two-column grid: serif numbers on the left, sans titles on the right with hairlines spanning only the title column. Most minimal.
  • D Spread420 px · italic Magazine table-of-contents treatment. Deep top margin, italic EB Garamond, Roman-numeral past list with dotted leaders and page folios.
  • E Bold accordion360 px · inter · click to expand Bold sans titles with hairline rules between rows. Click a past row to expand and see the people who answered, listed by ID in two quiet columns.
