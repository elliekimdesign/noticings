Zoom control — 6 minimal variants

All non-slider directions. None of them is a draggable bar like the current vertical zoom lever. Each tries a different metaphor — typographic, rhythmic, instrumental.

ATwo mono buttons · − zoom +
Just two clickable characters, with the current zoom level printed between. Quietest, very editorial.
12.5
BFive-dot stepper
5 small dots representing zoom levels. Click any dot to set zoom; the active one is bigger and full opacity.
CStacked +/− framed
A single small frame with + on top and − on bottom, divided by a line. Map-control classic but minimal mono.
DHorizontal tick scale
5 vertical ticks with thin connecting lines, like a survey marker. Active tick is taller. Clickable to change zoom level.
ENumeric scale
Big legible zoom number with tiny up/down arrows underneath. Reads like a dashboard / instrument panel.
12.5 zoom
FRuler with diamond head
Tiny horizontal ruler (5 marks + line) with a diamond-shaped indicator. Survey-instrument feel.
