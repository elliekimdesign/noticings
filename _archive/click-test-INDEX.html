Click state options for the freehand circle

Same default mark (the freehand wobbly circle), four different "active" treatments. Click each to compare how the open→active transition feels.

  • AFills with orange ink
    Same wobbly outline; the inside fills with orange. Reads as "the mark is now alive." Visually rhymes with the orange answer pins on the map.
  • BSecond circle traces over it
    A second freehand circle is drawn over the first, slightly different. Like "looking again, looking harder." Hand-drawn through and through.
  • CStroke thickens / presses harder
    The stroke gets bolder, like the pen pressed harder. Most subtle. Tactile, almost feels like clicking down a pen.
  • DSmall dash appears below
    The circle stays the same. A small hand-drawn dash appears below it, like a quiet underline / selection mark.
