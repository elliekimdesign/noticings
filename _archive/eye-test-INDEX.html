Eye toggle — icon options

Seven directions for the spotlight-mode toggle. Each opens its own preview page — click to compare. The icon sits in the same top-right position and at the same real size as it would on the live page.

  • AVictorian pointing hand
    Reuses the hand-drawn hand already used for the pending pin marker. Most consistent with the project's visual language.
  • BMagnifying glass
    Hand-drawn line magnifier. Says "look closer" without the surveillance feeling of an eye.
  • CShutter / slats
    Two pairs of vertical slats. Active state: slats spread apart, like peeking through a window.
  • DTheatre curtain
    Small curtain. Active state: curtains pull aside. Theatrical, fits the "small private window" idea.
  • EConstellation
    Three loose dots. Active state: thin lines connect them into a constellation. Says "the dots connect now."
  • FSun → moon
    Small sun with rays. Active state: morphs into a crescent moon. Most universally legible.
  • GBinoculars
    Line-drawn binoculars. Charming, distinctive, says "looking from afar at strangers' lives."
