Four answer sheet directions

Each link opens a static mockup of the answer sheet in a different artistic direction. Look at all four, pick your favorite — then I'll wire it into the live drawing-v13.html.

Direction A

Wall Text

Lawrence Weiner · Jenny Holzer
No box, no border. The answer becomes oversized Fraunces text floating directly on the canvas — like a gallery wall declaration. Bold, statement-driven.

Direction B

Color Field

Ellsworth Kelly · Rothko · Bauhaus
The sheet IS a color — a saturated gold block with white text inside. The submit button is a contrasting dark block tucked into one corner.

Direction C

Grid & Silence

Agnes Martin
A faint 3×3 gold grid divides the sheet. Each piece of content lives in its own cell — most cells stay empty on purpose. Quiet, meditative.

Direction D

Concrete Poetry

Ed Ruscha
Asymmetric typographic composition. The location is HUGE Fraunces breaking the edge. The input sits offset like a line of verse. The button is just "pin." as final punctuation.
